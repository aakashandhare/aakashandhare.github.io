About Me

A TA leader who has seen hiring from every angle.

I've spent 15 years in Talent Acquisition — starting on the staffing and RPO side before moving into senior TA leadership across corporate, GCC, and enterprise environments. That journey gives me something most TA leaders don't have: perspective from both sides of the desk.


I've functioned as an embedded TA partner for 50+ global organisations in Hyderabad — from initial workforce planning through to offer management — across 9 industries. The difference between my experience and someone who has only hired for one company is breadth. I've solved the same core hiring challenges across dozens of different contexts, which means I bring tested solutions, not experiments.


For the past 10+ years I've led high performance TA teams — building them from scratch, developing talent within them, and keeping attrition below 5% while consistently hitting delivery targets. I believe the best TA leaders don't just hire well — they build teams that hire well without them.


With 15 years of building, leading, and transforming TA functions across Hyderabad's most demanding talent markets — I bring tested solutions, deep relationships, and a genuine passion for making hiring work better.
